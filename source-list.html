Tampa Bay Girls Hockey — Source List
RESEARCH REFERENCE · LAST UPDATED APRIL 2026

How to Use This

This is the master list of every website, social account, league, and organization to check when doing a monthly event sweep for the Tampa Bay Girls Hockey Hub. Sources are grouped by type with check frequency and specific tips for what to look for at each one. High priority sources post the most events and should never be skipped.

Ice Rinks
PRIMARY EVENT VENUES
AdventHealth Center Ice
HIGH
Largest ice complex in the Southeast — 5 rinks in Wesley Chapel. Home of Tampa Bay Crunch. Hosts the most girls hockey activity of any single facility in the region, including tryouts, tournaments, and clinics.
↗ ahcenterice.com FB IG
Check: Events calendar page + Facebook for clinic/tryout posts between rink orgs.
TGH Ice Plex (Brandon)
HIGH
Official training facility of the Tampa Bay Lightning. Two-sheet rink in Brandon offering youth leagues, camps, clinics, and instructional programs. Formerly Brandon Ice Sports Forum.
↗ tghiceplex.com FB IG
Check: Clinics/Camps page. Lightning Made programs often show here first.
Power Pole Arena (XTRA Ice)
HIGH
Tampa rink and primary home for Lightning Girls Only programming — Learn to Play, LTP 201, rec leagues, and ball hockey. Coached by Kelley Steadman and Lightning staff. Girls-focused pipeline starts here.
↗ xtraicer.com ↗ Lightning Girls Page
Check: Lightning Hockey Development girls page — new sessions and LTP 201 dates post there before the rink site.
Clearwater Ice Arena
MED
13940 Icot Blvd, Clearwater. Used for Lightning Girls High School Hockey evaluation skates (starts March) and occasional clinics. Part of Tampa Bay Ice / TBSA family.
↗ tampabayice.com
Check: Tampa Bay Ice camps page. Girls HS Hockey eval skates run here each spring.
Tampa Bay Skating Academy – Oldsmar
MED
255 Forest Lakes Blvd, Oldsmar. Home base for Tampa Bay Hockey Club (TBHC). Hosts youth leagues, tryouts, and instructional programs. Part of Tampa Bay Ice family.
↗ tampabayice.com
Check: TBHC website for tryout/event dates since they use this rink.
Lightning Made Training Center
HIGH
6910 Asphalt Ave, Tampa. Indoor facility with synthetic ice and ball hockey rinks. Core hub for Lightning Girls Only pipeline including ball hockey leagues, Learn to Skate, and beginner/rec level girls teams. Nearly 200 girls participate.
↗ LMTC Page
Check: Lightning Hockey Development site for new session registration. Kelley Steadman posts updates directly.
Teams & Organizations
TRYOUTS · ROSTERS · PROGRAMS
Tampa Bay Lightning – Girls Hockey Dev
HIGH
The central hub for all Lightning-run girls programs: Learn to Play, LTP 201, Girls Only Camps/Clinics, Rec Leagues, Girls Jr. Lightning travel teams (10U–19U AA), and Girls High School Hockey. Coordinated by Kelley Steadman.
↗ Girls Page ↗ Girls Camps/Clinics
Contact: Kelley Steadman — KSteadman@viniksportsgroup.com. Best single point of contact in Tampa Bay girls hockey.
Tampa Bay Crunch (Girls)
HIGH
Tier 2 travel program based at AdventHealth Center Ice. Fields Girls 12UA, 14UA, and 16UA teams. Now partnered with Tampa Bay Lightning. 2026-27 Girls tryouts beginning May, exact dates posted to FB/Instagram first.
↗ AHCI Site FB IG @tampabay_crunch
Social media posts tryout info weeks before the website updates. Follow both FB and IG closely in March–May.
Tampa Bay Hockey Club (TBHC)
MED
Tier 2 org based in Oldsmar (TBSA rink) since 1987. Co-ed but runs girls-eligible teams. 2026-27 Fall Season Tryouts begin May 4. Worth monitoring for girls-eligible slots and any girls-only events.
↗ tampabayhc.com IG @tampabayhockeyclub
Check the "Upcoming Events" section on homepage — it updates with tryout windows.
Tampa Bay Jr. Lightning (Girls Tier 2 AA)
HIGH
Brand new program launched Feb 2026 — the Lightning's first direct operation of a competitive travel program. Girls Tier 2 AA overseen by Aaron Humphrey and Kelley Steadman. Training at AdventHealth Center Ice. Tryout dates for girls TBD.
↗ Jr. Lightning Page FB Girls Team FB Group
Very new — monitor closely. Girls details described as "coming soon" as of April 2026. Email: JrLightningGirlsHockey@gmail.com
Florida Alliance Girls Hockey
MED
Statewide elite/national-bound girls teams at 10U–19U, operated under SAHOF. Includes Tampa Bay Lightning Elite Girls team. Tracks team rosters, schedules, and standings for top-tier Florida girls hockey.
↗ floridaalliancegirlshockey.com
Check for tryouts posted by individual teams — Lightning Elite Girls is the main Tampa affiliate.
Leagues & Governing Bodies
SCHEDULES · STANDINGS · STATE EVENTS
SAHOF – Statewide Amateur Hockey of Florida
HIGH
Governing body for all youth and adult amateur hockey in Florida. Manages SFHL (boys) and SFGHL (girls). Posts state championship qualifications, district events, and girls hockey development news.
↗ sahofhockey.org ↗ Girls Page
Check Girls page for state championship qualifications, SGHL updates, and Florida Alliance news.
Southern Girls Hockey League (SGHL)
MED
First girls-only youth hockey league in the Southeast. Divisions at 8/10U dev, 12U, 16U, and 19U. Florida teams compete against Carolina, Georgia, Tennessee. Tampa Lightning teams play in this league.
↗ via SAHOF ✉ Email League
Contact directly for schedule releases and new team announcements each season.
USA Hockey – Southeastern District
WATCH
National governing body. Runs Learn to Play programs, national championship qualifications, and player development events. Tampa Bay is in the Southeastern District.
↗ usahockey.com ↗ Lightning LTP
Check for national camp/clinic announcements and any Southeastern District girls events.
Statewide Florida Girls Hockey League (SFGHL)
WATCH
Tier 2 AA and A-level girls league operated by SAHOF. Tampa teams play here. Schedules and standings available through MYHockey Rankings and SFHL Hockey site.
↗ sfhlhockey.com
Check for new season scheduling and Tampa-area girls team rosters each August.
Tournament Organizers
GIRLS DIVISIONS CONFIRMED
TCS Hockey – Tampa Memorial Cup
HIGH
Annual tournament at AdventHealth Center Ice. 2026 dates: May 22–25. Girls divisions at 10U, 12U, 14U, and 16U/19U. One of the biggest Tampa-hosted girls tournaments of the year.
↗ Tampa Memorial Cup ↗ tcshockey.com
Check annually in Jan–Feb for registration open dates. Girls divisions always included.
National Girls Hockey League (NGHL)
MED
Tampa Bay Crunch is a member. Posts schedules, standings, and league tournament info for girls travel teams across the Southeast.
↗ Crunch at NGHL ↗ nghlhockey.com
Check for schedule releases at the start of each season and any showcase/tournament events hosted in Florida.
Hockey Finder – Tournament Listings
WATCH
Aggregator for youth hockey tournaments across the US. Search by state/region to catch any girls tournaments in Florida not promoted by individual orgs.
↗ hockeyfinder.com
Filter by Florida + girls. Good for catching regional events that route through Tampa-area rinks.

Known Gaps — Sources Still Needed

  • Clearwater Ice Arena does not maintain a reliable events calendar — best reached by phone (727) 536-5843 or through Tampa Bay Ice email
  • Power Pole Arena event listings are inconsistent online — Lightning Dev site and Kelley Steadman are more reliable than the rink itself
  • Small-group private clinics and stick-and-puck sessions are almost exclusively posted to Facebook community groups — identify and join Tampa Bay youth hockey parent groups
  • Ellenton Ice and Sports Complex (Manatee County) is close enough to draw Tampa families for clinics — worth adding to future sweeps
  • Building a community submission form on the site is the best long-term fix for catching social-media-only events
